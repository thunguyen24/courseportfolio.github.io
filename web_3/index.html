Thoughts on
this course

It’s a language you will need to learn, and type various “sentences” or commands in that language. Yes, theoretically, once you learn a programming language, you do possess the language syntax you need to program anything you’d like under and above the sun. But is that really what you need? Think of it this way. In your native language you talk to with your buddies or family, what is the goal when you formulate a sentence? To put the right words in the sentence, or to get your message across the right way? I am sure that many times you’ve said something and then you had to explain it, so that people actually understood what you meant. Same goes for programming languages. I've thought so much about this since the beginning of this course. And now, after 1 month, I still appreciate it. I'm thankful for this course happened. I had chances to learn, practice and creative.
